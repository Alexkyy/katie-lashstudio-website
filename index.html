Meet Your Artist

Hi, I'm Katie

I have over eight years of experience in the beauty industry. I'm a certified lash artist based in Lynnwood, WA, passionate about helping every client feel their most beautiful and confident. I believe lashes should look natural, feel weightless, and last as long as possible.

I specialize in Classic, Hybrid, and Korean lash lifts, carefully tailoring each service to your unique eye shape, lash health, and daily lifestyle. My studio is a calm, welcoming space where you can relax and trust you're in skilled hands.

I use only premium lashes and professional-grade adhesives to ensure your comfort and the longevity of every set.
